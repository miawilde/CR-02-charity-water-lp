Our Story

Founded in 2006 by Scott Harrison—a former NYC nightclub promoter turned humanitarian after volunteering on Mercy Ships—charity: water began with a bold idea: fund clean water projects in developing nations using social and innovative methods From a launch party that raised $15,000 for African wells, they grew through viral grassroots efforts like “donate your birthday”—encouraging supporters to ask for donations instead of gifts . Their ethos of radical transparency means every public dollar funds projects directly, with GPS coordinates and photos shared publicly

Real Impact: Meet Amina

Amina used to walk miles every day to collect water for her family. Thanks to your support, her village now has a clean water well. Amina can go to school and her family is healthier and happier.
